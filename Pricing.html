Pricing FAQs

What are your transaction fees?

Our pricing structure is primarily based on products and features utilized. We do not charge per transaction fees nor basis points per transaction.

Do you sit in the flow of funds?

No. Modern Treasury does not hold your funds in our bank accounts or route transactions by touching your money. When you initiate a payment order, for instance, we are sending an instruction to your underlying bank partner. We are an independent software layer built on top of your bank partner.

What kinds of companies use Modern Treasury?

Modern Treasury works with companies from the Fintech 50 list to Fortune 500: Fast-growing companies: Our bank payments and ledgering APIs are used by startups to launch products that move money, from lending to digital wallets to programmatic payouts. Large complex enterprises: Our reconciliation engine, robust controls, and bank network remove the pain of dealing with multiple rails, banks, and payment processes. If you are facing complexities in money movement, be it because money is a core part of your product or service or because of your size, we can help. For more information on how we work with customers, you can review our case studies here.

What if my needs have changed after a contract is closed?

We are happy to work with you to ensure pricing reflects your current needs.

How does customer support work at Modern Treasury?

Payments are complex and hard to navigate. For that reason, quality in customer service is core to the work we do. Customers are assigned a customer success manager to handle go-live and day-to-day needs. We also offer optional professional implementation services for customers with complex use cases.
